Research

  • ▶ The Macroeconomic Effects of Defence Expenditure: Evidence from Spain — Co-authored with Mario Alloza, Rubén Dominguez and Iacopo Varotto (Working Paper) [PDF]
    This paper studies the macroeconomic effects of defence expenditure in Spain using narrative and econometric identification strategies. We estimate fiscal multipliers and assess heterogeneity across business cycle phases and financing conditions.
  • ▶ The Economic Impact of the European Single Market — Co-authored with Paolo Pasimeni (Single Market Economic Papers, DG GROW) [PDF]
    This report quantifies the economic gains from the European Single Market, focusing on trade, productivity, and investment effects across member states and sectors.
  • ▶ EU-China FDI: recent trends and implications on EU exposure — Co-authored with Frank Vandermeeren (Single Market Economic Papers, DG GROW) [PDF]
    This paper documents recent trends in EU–China foreign direct investment and discusses implications for EU strategic dependencies, sectoral exposure, and policy responses.
