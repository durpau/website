Market Power and Innovation: Evidence from Concentrated Industries

P. Durá Díez, A. Gómez — Journal of Industrial Economics, 2024

We investigate the relationship between market concentration and firm-level R&D using firm-level panel data. Results suggest that moderate concentration can increase innovation incentives in the short run.

Entry Deterrence and Regulation in Two-Sided Markets

P. Durá Díez — Review of Economic Studies (Working Paper), 2023

This paper models entry deterrence in platform markets and studies how regulation changes pricing incentives and welfare.
